4. Privacy Architecture

This service is designed with privacy as a core principle:

How Your Data Flows

All communication is encrypted using TLS 1.2/1.3:

  1. Your browser sends an encrypted request to this proxy (HTTPS)
  2. This proxy validates and forwards to Venice.ai (HTTPS) without logging content
  3. Venice.ai processes without storing prompts or responses
  4. Response streams back through the proxy directly to your browser
  5. Conversation saved only in your browser's local storage

What We DO NOT Collect

  • Your prompts or messages
  • AI responses
  • Conversation history
  • Personal information

What We DO Collect

  • IP addresses (temporary, in-memory, for rate limiting)
  • Model selection per request
  • Request timestamps
  • Error logs (without conversation content)

Venice.ai's Privacy Guarantee: Venice.ai does not store or log any prompts or model responses on their servers. Your conversation history exists only in your local browser storage. For full details, see Venice.ai's Privacy Architecture.

Infrastructure Note: This service runs on Vercel's Edge Runtime. While Vercel provides the hosting infrastructure, they do not log request or response bodies by default. All data in transit is encrypted with TLS. For details, see Vercel's Security Documentation.
